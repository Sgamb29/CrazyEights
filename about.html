Crazy Eights: How To Play

Play!

The goal of the game is to be the first player with 0 cards.

The current main card is in the center of the table and on your turn you can either play a card of the same suit or the same number as the main card.

If you cannot play then you draw a card from the deck that is beside the main card.

Special Cards:

2's: Makes the other person pick up 2 cards. If the other player puts a 2 on top of your two than you have to pick up two cards times how many two's are there (2, 4, 6, 8).
Jacks: Makes the other player skip their turn.
Eights: A wild card, you can play it anytime and change the suit to whatever you like.
